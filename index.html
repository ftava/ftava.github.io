ABOUT ME


I am an engineer and a consultant currently living and working in the Silicon Valley. My professional experience is a combination of technical and business-oriented jobs in government and start-up settings. Previously I was a product manager and design develop engineer for Maplex, a Mobile App development startup company. I am broadly interested in the design and analysis of smart cities, infrastructures, energy efficient houses, and more generally Theoretical Structures and Computer Science. Some specific projects and corporations that I have worked with during and after my undergrad includes the Stanford University Research Park, US Department of Transportation, and Arup (read: Silicon Valley Growth). Seismic Waves

One of my latest projects and experiments in programing is a combination of collecting data and visualizations, written in the Python language. Although the program is still in its early stages, we hope it would be used for software development, personal information, and study purposes.
